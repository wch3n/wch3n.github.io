<section class="section-block" id="research">
  <div class="section-heading">
    <div>
      <p class="eyebrow">Research</p>
      <h2>From electronic structure to materials discovery</h2>
    </div>
    <p>
      I work at the intersection of quantum-mechanical accuracy, defect physics, and data-intensive exploration of complex materials spaces.
    </p>
  </div>

  <div class="section-grid">
    <article class="section-card">
      <h3>Electronic-structure theory</h3>
      <p>
        Development and assessment of hybrid density functionals, dielectric-dependent approaches, GW methods, and vertex corrections for condensed-matter systems.
      </p>
      <ul class="pill-list">
        <li>Hybrid functionals</li>
        <li>GW</li>
        <li>Vertex corrections</li>
      </ul>
    </article>

    <article class="section-card">
      <h3>Defects and quantum states</h3>
      <p>
        First-principles analysis of point defects, band-edge alignment, and optically active centers in semiconductors and low-dimensional materials.
      </p>
      <ul class="pill-list">
        <li>Defect levels</li>
        <li>Semiconductors</li>
        <li>Quantum defects</li>
      </ul>
    </article>

    <article class="section-card">
      <h3>High-throughput discovery</h3>
      <p>
        Computational screening pipelines for ferromagnetic semiconductors, high-entropy alloys, and materials with targeted electronic or thermodynamic behavior.
      </p>
      <ul class="pill-list">
        <li>Materials screening</li>
        <li>Big-data thermodynamics</li>
        <li>Workflow automation</li>
      </ul>
    </article>

    <article class="section-card">
      <h3>Energy materials</h3>
      <p>
        Theory-driven studies of kesterites, photocatalytic materials, perovskites, and related systems relevant to photovoltaics and sustainable energy conversion.
      </p>
      <ul class="pill-list">
        <li>Photovoltaics</li>
        <li>Photocatalysis</li>
        <li>Complex oxides</li>
      </ul>
    </article>
  </div>
</section>

<section class="section-block" id="software">
  <div class="section-heading">
    <div>
      <p class="eyebrow">Software</p>
      <h2>Code development for scientific research</h2>
    </div>
    <p>
      I contribute to both large community codes and focused research tools, with emphasis on methods that are robust enough for production-scale studies.
    </p>
  </div>

  <div class="software-grid">
    <article class="section-card">
      <h3>GCMC</h3>
      <p>
        Grand Canonical Monte Carlo code with replica exchange and hybrid MC/MD support for ASE-compatible machine-learned interatomic potentials.
      </p>
      <ul class="pill-list">
        <li>ASE</li>
        <li>Replica exchange</li>
        <li>MLIPs</li>
      </ul>
    </article>

    <article class="section-card">
      <h3>ABINIT</h3>
      <p>
        Contributions spanning exchange-correlation kernels, vertex corrections, and method development for high-accuracy electronic-structure calculations.
      </p>
      <ul class="pill-list">
        <li>Many-body methods</li>
        <li>Method development</li>
        <li>Production code</li>
      </ul>
    </article>

    <article class="section-card">
      <h3>Quantum ESPRESSO</h3>
      <p>
        Implementation work on range-separated hybrid functionals and related capabilities for plane-wave first-principles simulations.
      </p>
      <ul class="pill-list">
        <li>Range-separated hybrids</li>
        <li>Plane waves</li>
        <li>First principles</li>
      </ul>
    </article>

    <article class="section-card">
      <h3>Research tooling</h3>
      <p>
        Python packages and web tooling including FNV for charged defect corrections and alloy-stability platforms for high-throughput prediction.
      </p>
      <ul class="pill-list">
